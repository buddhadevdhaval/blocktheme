What is
genetic testing?

Genetic testing studies your genes. Your genes make you who you are, and that includes aspects of your health. Genetic testing can shed light on risks for inherited disease that you may have, like cancer or rare disease.

Why have
genetic testing?

Depending on the results, genetic testing can help you make informed health decisions that impact you and your family. Genetic testing may make choices and actions clearer, guiding you and your healthcare provider on how to move forward.

How do I get
genetic testing?

Talking to your healthcare provider is the first step. They may refer you to a genetic counselor, a specialized healthcare professional, to talk about genetic testing and how it might fit into your family's health history.
