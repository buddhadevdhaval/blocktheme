How do I select and order testing?

Ambry's simple and intuitive online ordering system (AmbryPort) helps you choose and order the right test. Throughout the process, our team is here to support and serve you.

Step 1
Order a Sample Kit
Get your hands on a sample kit from AmbryPort.
Step 2
Ship sample to Ambry
Collect samples per instructions depending on the sample kit.
Step 3
Lab completes testing
Once the test is received, the lab completes testing depending on the test ordered.
Step 4
Results available!
Test Results are then available on AmbryPort.
