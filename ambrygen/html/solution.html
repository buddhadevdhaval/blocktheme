Patient for Life Program

Our promise to patients living with rare and undiagnosed conditions, today and in the future. Ambry continuously reviews data for potential pathogenic or likely pathogenic variants in newly characterized genes. As applicable, we proactively issue reclassification result reports.

Family Studies

Clarifying genetic variants of uncertain significance (VUS) gives meaningful clinical insights to you and your patients. Our Family Studies Program with follow-up testing of appropriate family members can provide valuable information in this process.
